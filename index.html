About Us

How we started

A passion project made entirely by two friends that are completely astonished by the potential of the blockchain technology. Inspired by its everlasting capabilities, we decided to bring onto the Ethereum blockchain our Sad MeowMeows kitties.

We bring you a collection of the cutest 10000 randomly generated Ethereum Blockchain ERC-721 NFTs, assembled from over 400 possible properties and a super special edition of 10 rare NFTs, and supported by the decentralized IPFS.

Besides this, our main objective is to create a familiar, friendly and welcoming community with its focus on learning with each other so you can too contribute to the NFT community in your own special way! With that said, we'll continue to study and explore new ways to elevate and push this project forward!
